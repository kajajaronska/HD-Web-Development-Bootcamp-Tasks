Timeline of his life:

Below you can find a list of key moments in Thomas Edison's life.

  • 1847 Born on Febraury 11 in Milan, Ohio.
  • 1859 Starts a job selling newspapers and candy on the Grand Trunk Railway.
  • 1862 Starts working as a telegraph operator in Port Huron.
  • 1869 Receives first patent for his first invention - anelectric vote recorder
  • 1877 Invents carbon telephone transmitter, which extended the clarity and range of the telephone.
  • 1878-79 His most popular invention takes place:electric incandescent light bulb that lasted for morethan 13 hours.
  • 1882 Opens a commercial electric station in New York City with approx. 85 customers.
  • 1889 Creates General Electric in April and produces dolls with tiny cylinders inside to make them talk.
  • 1892 Edison General Electric and Thomson-Houston merge into General Electric.
  • 1898 Spanish-American War begins; Edison Company sends cameramen to Cuba to film scenes of war.
  • 1923 Works on finding alternatives sources of rubber.
  • 1931 Dies October 18 in West Orange, New Jersey.
